About Medcare

Medcare integrates the Kenyatta Hospital and Aga Khan Hospital to give you the best care.

Our Mission

Advancing Health Together.

As a world-class academic and health care system, Medcare Health strives to transform medicine and health locally and globally through innovative scientific research, rapid translation of breakthrough discoveries, educating future clinical and scientific leaders, advocating and practicing evidence-based medicine to improve community health, and leading efforts to eliminate health inequalities.

Our Vission

In advancing health together, we will:

  • Deliver tomorrow's health care today
  • Connect with the world to improve health globally
  • Accelerate discovery and its translation
  • Build healthy communities
  • Create education that is transforming

Our Core Values

putting patients first and treating everyone with dignity and respect.

Medcare is a comprehensive healthcare network featuring two hospitals, two urgent cares, an emergent care center, outpatient services and a provider network within Nairobi and beyond.

We believe that caring can't be taught, kindness isn't a subject learned in medical school, and compassion doesn't come with a degree; these intrinsic qualities are deeply rooted in our culture, which can be seen, heard, and felt throughout our system. We're big enough to provide exceptional care, yet small enough to keep it personalized; we provide healthcare that actually cares.
